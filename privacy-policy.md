# Privacy Policy for My Paris

Last updated: February 2026

My Paris ("the App") is a personal memory and travel logging application developed by an individual developer. Your privacy is important, and this policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

---

## Information We Collect

The App itself does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Any data you create or save in the App is stored **locally on your device only**.

---

## Location Information

The App may request access to your location **only while the App is in use**.

This is used solely to:
- Save visited places on a map
- Attach geographic context to your personal memories

Location data is **not collected, stored externally, or shared** with the developer or any third parties.

---

## Photos and Media

The App may request access to your photo library to allow you to:
- Select photos
- Attach images to your saved memories

Photos are used **only within the App** and remain on your device.  
The App does not upload, analyze, or share your photos.

---

## Data Sharing

The App:
- Does **not** use analytics services
- Does **not** include advertising
- Does **not** share data with third parties

---

## Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

---

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if the App’s functionality changes.  
Any updates will be reflected on this page.

---

## Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer via the App Store listing.
